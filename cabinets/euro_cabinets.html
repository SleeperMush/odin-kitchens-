European Style Cabinets

Photo by Metropolis Drafting and Construction Inc - More kitchen ideas
Modern elegance exemplified in European tastes

European style cabinets, sometimes known as Scandanavian cabinets due to their IKEA induced popularity in the American market, are one of major style considerations if one is aiming for a contemporary kitchen. Dating back to the Bauhaus movement in Germany during the mid 20th century, European cabinets aimed for simplicity and functionality over excessive design and ornate styles. European style cabinets aim for elegance through simplicity, sophistication through cleanliness, and functionality through ingenuity. They feature a framless design, different from tradition French Country style kitchens, and more minimalism than elegan Italian style kitchens. Another great feature of European cabinets is their range of design options. A designer can achieve warm wood tones, or cooler industrial tones based on the hardware and materials preferred. They are a fantastic selection for modern kitchens aiming for organization, cleanliness, and peace of mind.
